Help

This page provides basic support content for the prototype, including shipping, returns, and contact details.

Frequently asked questions

How does shipping work?

Shipping options are selected during checkout. Free shipping may apply based on the order total, and the summary panel updates automatically.

Can I change quantities in the cart?

Yes. The cart supports quantity changes and recalculates totals instantly to keep the checkout flow consistent.

What is the returns policy?

This is a prototype. For a real store, returns would include conditions and time frames. Here we show the content structure only.
