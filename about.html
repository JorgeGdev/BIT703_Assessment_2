About

Aotearoa Adventure Gear is a simple ecommerce prototype focused on outdoor equipment. This page explains the brand story and what the store offers.

Our story

We created this store concept to support hikers, campers, and climbers who want reliable gear. The goal is a clean, simple shopping experience with clear product information and a smooth checkout flow.

What we sell

  • Backpacks, tents, and sleeping bags for multi day trips
  • Jackets and layers for changing weather
  • Trekking poles, boots, and essentials for trail comfort

Design approach

The layout follows the provided wireframes and uses semantic HTML for structure. Tailwind CSS is used to keep spacing and typography consistent across pages.
